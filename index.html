Apa itu SDG?

SDG dibagi menjadi 17 tujuan yaitu; no poverty, zero hunger, good health and well-being, quality education, gender equality, clean water and sanitation, affordable and clean energy, decent work and economic growth, industry, innovation, and infrastructure, reduced inequalities, sustainable cities and communities, responsible consumption and production, climate action, life below water, life on land, peace and justice strong institutions, dan partnerships for the goals. Tujuan-tujuan ini bersifat berkelanjutan dan mengupayakan masa depan kita.
